CYBERNETICS [2022]

I designed, styled, edited, and modeled in this spread for my school's fashion magazine, Infinite Magazine Issue X. The concept of the spread was the nuances of everchanging technology and the line between human-technology interactions.

This spread was heavily inspired by the 80s cyberpunk movement as well as the book Electronic Superhighway by Omar Kholeif. This project made the cover of the magazine.

I dug out used electronic parts from the Stata Center dumpsters as well as parts from my microcomputer project class. Most editing was done in Lightroom and Photoshop; some images were generated/manipulated via Python code.
