ABOUT
I am a current freshman at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, with an intended double major in Computer Science and Mathematics.

A 2019 graduate from Poolesville High School's Science, Math, and Computer Science magnet program, I am interested in combining artificial intelligence to solve global healthcare issues. I am currently working on Health Analytics and Integrated Healthcare at IBM Research, and I have previously worked in AI for healthcare at both the Fondazione Bruno Kessler in Trento, Italy as well as at the George Washington University in Washington, D.C.

Outside of academics, I am interested in investigating the intersection of art, media, and technology through mediums such as graphic design and fashion. At MIT, I am currently involved in Women Business Leaders, StartLabs, Infinite Magazine, Machine Intelligence Community, and Mirchi. I have also learned competitive classical piano and Bharatanatyam (Indian classical dance) for 14 years.
